Types of Oils

Here are a few types of oils (some you may be familiar with)

Cedarwood
  • Helps prevent dry skin due to its anti-fungal properties.
  • Deals with dandruff by increasing circulation to the scalp. This can be added to hair oil, shampoo or directly on.
  • It helps with sore muscles by tightening as well as preventing loose skin.
Lime
  • It is an antiseptic, so it can prevent infections in cuts and wounds.
  • It can be used as a cleaning agent and take care of fungus and mold.
  • It can be used in your hand sanitizer
Lemongrass
  • A great bug repellent
  • It containts properties that can help improve the quality of your skin. This can be added to soaps, lotions, shampoos, etc.
  • It helps reduce odor and could be added to shoes, laundry, even your trash can.
Lavender
  • Helps improve mood by relieving anxiety and stress. It also helps with brain health by improving mental focus
  • It eases headaches and promotes sleep
  • It helps soothes sunburns
Peppermint
  • Relieves sore muscles by adding it to heat packs, salves or just directly applying it to the skin
  • It helps clear sinuses and loosen congestion. Use it during the holidays like Christmas!
  • It relieves nausea or upset stomach

Some oils to Avoid

  • Horseradish
  • Pennyroyal
  • Rue
  • Sassfras
  • Boldo
  • Bitter Almond
  • Wormwood

Carrier Oils

When applying an essential oil on topically, you must dilute it with a carrier oil first. This will prevent irritation or other side effects.

  • Avocado Oil
  • Coconut Oil
  • Grapeseed
  • Almond
  • Jojoba oil

Recipes

Here are some recipes that I personally use (using online sources),
however most of the time I use an oil diffuser.

Bad Breath

Add a drop of peppermint oil to your toothpaste

Itchy Scalp

A drop of lavendar, basil, or cedarwood on your scalp will help soothe itchiness

Blisters

Mix a few drops of tea tree oil with a carrier oil of your choice: Almond, Jojoba, Grapeseed, Coconut Oil, and Avocado Oil. Apply the mixture onto the area several times a day.

Coughs

Mix two drops of eucaluptus oil with a carrier oil: Almond, Jojoba, Grapeseed, Coconut oil, or Avocado Oil. Rub the mixture on your chest.
